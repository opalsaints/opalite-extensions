## GEMINI SUITE

### Short Description (132 chars max)
Save AI-generated images from Gemini to your Opalite cloud library. Organize, upscale, and share from one dashboard.

### Detailed Description
Gemini Suite by Opalite is the easiest way to save, organize, and enhance AI-generated images from Google Gemini.

WHAT IT DOES
Gemini Suite automatically detects images you create with Google Gemini, then saves them to your Opalite cloud library — no manual downloading required.

KEY FEATURES
- One-Click Save: Images are detected and saved automatically as you generate them
- Cloud Library: All your AI images in one organized, searchable collection
- AI Upscaling: Enhance resolution up to 4x with built-in upscaler
- Easy Sharing: Share images directly from your Opalite dashboard
- Cross-Platform: Works across Google's AI image generation tools
- Real-Time Sync: Your library stays in sync across all your devices

HOW IT WORKS
1. Generate images on Google's AI platforms
2. Gemini Suite automatically detects and saves them to your Opalite library
3. Open your Opalite dashboard to organize, upscale, and share

GETTING STARTED
1. Install the extension
2. Sign in with your Opalite account (free to create)
3. Start generating images on Gemini — they'll be saved automatically

Your AI images deserve a proper home. Stop losing track of your creations across browser tabs and download folders. Gemini Suite keeps everything organized in one place.

Visit opalitestudios.com to learn more.

### Single Purpose Statement
Save and organize AI-generated images from Google Gemini into a cloud media library.

## PERMISSIONS JUSTIFICATIONS (shared across all 3)

### downloads
Required to save AI-generated images from the platform to the user's Opalite cloud library.

### declarativeNetRequest
Required to modify network request headers for proper communication with the Opalite API server and to handle cross-origin image fetching.

### storage
Required to persist user authentication state, extension preferences, and sync settings between browser sessions.

### notifications
Required to notify the user when images are successfully saved, when processing (upscaling) is complete, and for important account alerts.

### scripting
Required to inject content scripts into supported AI platforms to detect and capture AI-generated images from the page.

### activeTab
Required to interact with the current tab's content when the user activates the extension, enabling image detection on supported platforms.

### cookies
Required to maintain authenticated sessions with the Opalite API server (opalitestudios.com) for secure image upload and account management.

### Host Permissions Justifications

#### https://gemini.google.com/* (Gemini only)
Required to detect and capture AI-generated images on Google Gemini.

#### https://aistudio.google.com/* (Gemini only)
Required to detect and capture AI-generated images on Google AI Studio.

#### https://labs.google/* (Gemini only)
Required to detect and capture AI-generated images on Google Labs.

#### https://grok.com/* and https://*.grok.com/* (Grok only)
Required to detect and capture AI-generated images on xAI's Grok platform.

#### https://chatgpt.com/* and https://*.chatgpt.com/* (ChatGPT only)
Required to detect and capture AI-generated images on ChatGPT.

#### https://sora.chatgpt.com/* (ChatGPT only)
Required to detect and capture AI-generated videos on Sora.

#### https://opalitestudios.com/* and https://*.opalitestudios.com/*
Required for API communication, user authentication, and image upload to the Opalite cloud service.
