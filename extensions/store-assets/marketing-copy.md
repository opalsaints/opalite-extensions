## GEMINI SUITE by Opalite

### Short Description (132 chars max)
Auto-download AI images, batch prompts, video mode, reusable templates & more. The ultimate Gemini image workflow companion.

### Detailed Description

**Gemini Suite by Opalite** supercharges your Google Gemini image generation workflow with powerful automation tools — all from one sleek side panel.

**KEY FEATURES**

🎨 **Auto Download** — Automatically save every AI-generated image the moment it appears. No more right-click → save-as. Your creations go straight to your downloads folder.

📋 **Batch Prompts** — Queue up multiple prompts and send them one after another. Enter one prompt per line, hit Send, and watch Gemini work through your entire list.

🎬 **Video Mode** — Switch between TextToVideo, ImageToVideo, and ImageToText generation modes with a single click. Create AI videos directly from your prompts.

📝 **Prompt Templates** — Build reusable prompt templates with [Replacement] variables. Fill in the blanks, preview in real-time, and send with one click. Save hours on repetitive prompt crafting.

🔧 **Prompt Prefix / Suffix** — Automatically prepend or append text to every prompt. Set your style once ("cinematic lighting, 8K") and it applies to all generations.

📊 **Task Manager** — Track all your generation tasks with status monitoring, bulk actions, and export/import capabilities. Never lose track of a generation again.

🔄 **Variables System** — Create reusable prompt snippets (like camera settings or style descriptions) and insert them with @mentions. Combine variables with templates for infinite prompt combinations.

⚡ **Auto New Chat** — Automatically open a fresh chat for each generation to keep your workspace clean.

🎯 **Image & Prompt Matching** — Pair generated images with their source prompts for easy reference and iteration.

**HOW IT WORKS**
1. Install the extension and visit gemini.google.com
2. Click the Gemini Suite icon to open the side panel
3. Enter your prompts, toggle your preferred features, and hit Send
4. Images auto-download and tasks are tracked automatically

**SUPPORTED MODES**
• TextToVideo — Generate videos from text prompts
• ImageToVideo — Animate existing images into video
• ImageToText — Describe images with AI

Built by Opalite — making AI image workflows effortless.
